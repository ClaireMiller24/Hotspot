What we do

As fellow caffeine addicts and seekers of the best spots in Nottingham, we know how important it is for you to find the perfect cafe. A Cafe is more than just a place to get your daily drink fix. You could be looking for the best nest for working, a cosy chair to catch up with an old friend or a cute backdrop for a first date.

It can be daunting moving to a new city, let alone a new country. A welcoming cafe is the perfect place to start feeling more at home. Each one is unique, each one has a personality. The right cafe can put a warmth in your belly and a smile on your face. It can make a world of difference to your day.

Luckily, Nottingham has an abundance of cafes, each very different and answering to different needs. So how do you know which one is right for you?

We're here to help you find out.
